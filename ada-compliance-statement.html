ADA Compliance Statement

At Fleet Fuel Card, we are committed to ensuring that our website is accessible to everyone, including individuals with disabilities. We strive to meet the standards set forth by the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1, which outline how to make web content more accessible to people with a wide range of disabilities.

Our Accessibility Features:
  • Responsive Design: Our website is optimized for use on various devices and screen sizes, allowing for a seamless experience across desktops, tablets, and smartphones.
  • Keyboard Navigation: We ensure that all functionalities can be accessed via keyboard navigation for users who may not be able to use a mouse.
  • Alt Text for Images: Descriptive alternative text is provided for all significant images to ensure that visually impaired users using screen readers can access the information.
  • Text Resizing: Visitors can adjust text sizes using browser settings for ease of reading.
  • Color Contrast: We maintain high contrast between text and background to improve readability for users with visual impairments.
  • Accessible Forms: Our forms are designed to be navigable via assistive technology, providing labels and instructions to ensure clarity and ease of use.

Ongoing Efforts We are continually working to improve the accessibility of our website. Our team regularly monitors the website to identify any accessibility issues and updates the site to ensure compliance with the latest standards.
